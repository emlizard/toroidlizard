Formulas

Effective Area:
\( A_e = \frac{(OD - ID) \cdot h}{2} \)

Effective Length:
\( L_e = \pi \cdot \frac{OD - ID}{\ln(OD / ID)} \)

Effective Core Volume:
\( V_e = A_e \cdot L_e \)

AL (Inductance per turn):
\( AL = \mu_0 \cdot \mu_r \cdot \frac{A_e}{L_e} \cdot 10^6 \)[µH/N²]

Self-Inductance:
\( L = AL \cdot N^2 \)[µH]
