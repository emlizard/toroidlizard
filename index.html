Toroidal Core Calculator (with Gap)

Formulas (including gap)

Effective Area:
\( A_e = \frac{(OD - ID)\,h}{2} \)

Effective Length:
\( L_e = \pi \cdot \frac{OD - ID}{\ln\bigl(\frac{OD}{ID}\bigr)} \)

Effective Core Volume:
\( V_e = A_e \cdot L_e \)

AL (no gap):
\( A_L = \mu_0\,\mu_r \,\frac{A_e}{L_e} \times 10^9 \;\;[\text{nH}/N^2] \)

Inductance (no gap):
\( L = A_L \times N^2 \div 1000 \;\;[\mu H] \)

AL (with gap):
\( A_L = \frac{\mu_0\,A_e}{\frac{L_e}{\mu_r} + l_g} \times 10^9 \;\;[\text{nH}/N^2] \)

Inductance (with gap):
\( L = A_L \times N^2 \div 1000 \;\;[\mu H] \)

Here, \(l_g\) is the gap length in meters.
